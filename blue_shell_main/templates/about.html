About Blue Shell Amazon

Questions Might be Asked

Blue Shell Amazon is a mock site to Camelcamelcamel.com.

The high, low, current, and average prices in the product page are taken directly from Camelcamelcamel. Blue Shell Amazon uses these data to display the price comparison in a similar fashion but with a bar graph for simplicity. These services are free in Camelcamelcamel; therefore, to support it, the links to the products will be directed to Camelcamelcamel before landing at Amazon. Camelcamelcamel earn commission with every purchase made from their links and keeps their services ongoing.

Thank you for visiting Blue Shell Amazon.

This is explained in About section.
I used Seleninum webdriver to search and fetch the price of the product in Camelcamelcamel. The price fetching process takes few seconds before it gets rendered to the product page.
Unfortunately, there wasn't any other free resources of price records other than camelcamelcamel.com.
I tried to scrape data from Camelcamelcamel using BeautifulSoup; however, it returns 403 forbidden error. I am relying on BeautifulSoup to scrape the current price off of Amazon when price update is required.
The lowest price get updated after displaying the old values. Upon refresh, the lowest or highest prices will be updated accordingly.

When price record displays both Amazon and the 3rd party as sellers, and the seller is from Amazon, 3rd party prices are found in the other sellers shown in the bottom right corner.

Used prices are not displayed in Blue Shell Amazon.

The current prices are fetched from camelcamelcamel.com, but the prices sometimes are not updated especially if there are quick deals happening at the moment. So in certain cases another scraping is required to show the correct price.
Emails in Blue Shell Amazon are only used to deliver price alerts, your personal information will not be sold or shared outside of this website.
